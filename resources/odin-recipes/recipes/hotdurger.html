Hotdurger

DESCRIPTION:

Perhaps there's a theme here, two recipes that resemble the humble hot dog? Fear not, this is another winner!
Is it a hot dog or is it a hamburger? We still don't know what to classify it as, we've also been threatened with prison time if we ever serve this in public.

This recipe is a fan favourite of ours, simple yet elegant and packed with flavour! What more could you want?
I suppose there's nothing more to do than tell you how to replicate this in your own home, so instructions are as follows.

INGREDIENTS:

  • Hot dog roll (not stale or mouldy is preferred)
  • Burger pattie (beef preferrably, however we've had great success with room temperature 5 day old tuna)
  • Cheese sticks (the cheaper the better)
  • OPTIONAL: Sauce for flavour (we don't believe you'll need it though)

METHOD:

  1. Cry
  2. Tear hot dog bun open to make room for pattie
  3. Insert cheese sticks into roll
  4. Carefully add pattie in halves to roll, do this quietly to not attract evil spirits
  5. Serve on your filthiest plate with excess cheese sticks on the side
  6. OPTIONAL: Add sauce
  7. OPTIONAL: Call the police and confess your crimes

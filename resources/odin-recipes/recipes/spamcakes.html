Spamcakes

DESCRIPTION:

See, no theme here. This recipe dos not resemble a hot dog - in fact I wouldn't say it resembles food at all.
A recipe plucked from Grannie's book of wonders and recreated for your tastebuds pleasure, this recipe is sure to cause a stir!

For those of you who are food conscious, this recipe will ensure you meet your daily spam intake requirements.
Once again, the instructions to replicate this are as follows.

INGREDIENTS:

  • Pancakes (cooked, boneless)
  • Spam (aluminium can removed)
  • OPTIONAL: Gromit mug (highly recommended but not a necessity)

METHOD:

  1. Place cooked pancakes on a plate of your choosing
  2. Tear spam into manageable, bite sized pieces
  3. Place spam on pancakes
  4. Consume within 3-5 business days

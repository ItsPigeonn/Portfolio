Banana Dog

DESCRIPTION:

Eye catching, right? The banana dog is here to turn your mundane day on it's head!
Packed with potassium and loaded with sauce this meal is bound to have you begging for more!

But how can something so simple be so beautiful? You're asking the wrong questions here, your only question should be "how do I make this beauty?"

INGREDIENTS:

  • Banana (any species or size)
  • Tomato sauce (ketchup)
  • Mustard (not the gas)

METHOD:

  1. Peel a strip of the banana skin away from the goodness hidden within
  2. Generously apply your mustard layer
  3. Gently top the mustard layer with tomato sauce
  4. Serve without a plate, eat immediately to avoid the dish going cold (or hot)
